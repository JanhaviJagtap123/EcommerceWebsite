Who We Are?

Cara is a social commerce platform that undertakes retail distribution, enabling small retail merchants to connect and sell their products effectively via social media channels. Bengaluru-based Cara aims to create an environment where anyone can start their business with zero investment. The startup aims to create about 20 million micro-entrepreneurs. There are over two million resellers on the Cara platform and more than 20,000 manufacturers from more than 500 towns who get distribution through Cara. Cara works on approximately 10-15 percent commission and sellers can earn with Cara by adding a profit margin on every sale.
